Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian casserole made by layering meat-based tomato sauce, cheese (ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan), and pasta sheets, typically in a 9x13-inch pan. It is assembled by alternating these layers, starting with sauce at the bottom, then baked covered at for about 25-30 minutes, then uncovered until bubbly.
